What is Project Webinar
We are a meeting platform that allows hosts to create paid virtual meetings. Setting up an online payment system and managing customer payments is difficult so our goal is to give educators and influencers an easier way to host paid meetings
Project Webinar is a placeholder name
Who is our product for?
Our product is for anyone who wants to grow their audience by offering virtual meetings. Whether you’re an educator or influencer, we help you have access to more audience members while still making sure you are compensated
What is the process to hosting a meeting?
All you need to do is create an account with your email, start a meeting, select meeting preferences such as price per person, and create meeting. After that, you’ll be given a meeting link that you can share to members. They will be prompted to pay your fee once they go to that link
What are our fees?
Right now since we are still in beta we are not charging any fees! We just ask that you give us as much input as you can in how you think we could improve our product for you.
Why do we need early testers?
We would love any input we can get so we know where we can improve! Since we are still in beta there are many aspects of the product we are unsure of
Who are our founders?
Project webinar is founded by two college students who want to give anyone a platform to make money while spreading their knowledge and experiences. You can find our LinkedIn profiles and contact info to get in touch!
